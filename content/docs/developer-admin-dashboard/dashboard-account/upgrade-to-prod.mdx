## **Upgrade Procedure**

1. **Eligibility Check**:  
   * Ensure your integration passes all required tests  
   * Review and comply with Okto's production guidelines  
2. **Submit Upgrade Request**:  
   * Log into your Dashboard Account  
   * Navigate to [Upgrade to Prod](https://forms.google.com/) on the Dashboard Home Page.  
   * Fill out the upgrade request form  
3. **Verification Process**:  
   * Okto team reviews your application  
   * May require additional information or integration adjustments  
4. **Approval and Setup**:  
   * Upon approval, you'll receive instructions for production setup  
   * Update your integration with production API keys and endpoints  
5. **Go Live**:  
   * Perform final tests in the production environment  
   * Launch your Okto-integrated application
